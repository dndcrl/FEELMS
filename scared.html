Films for when you want to feel scared. Watch at your own risk.

Scary movies, also known as horror films, are designed to evoke fear, suspense, and anxiety in their audience through eerie atmospheres, unsettling visuals, and intense storytelling. These films often explore themes of the supernatural, psychological terror, monsters, or human depravity, creating an immersive experience that lingers in the mind long after the credits roll. Whether through sudden jump scares, haunting sound design, or deeply disturbing narratives, horror movies tap into primal fears, forcing viewers to confront the unknown, the grotesque, or the darkness within humanity.
